# Auto Uchytil — Business Description

## What They Do

Auto Uchytil is a Prague-based automotive consulting and pre-purchase inspection service that helps used car buyers make safe, informed purchases. They verify the technical condition of vehicles and negotiate better prices on behalf of their clients before any money changes hands.

## Services

### Vehicle Inspection — 3,890 CZK
A thorough on-site inspection covering engine and drivetrain, exterior and paintwork, brakes and suspension, electrical systems, interior and equipment, and documentation verification. Delivered as a written report with recommendations.

### Vehicle Search with Inspection — 4,900 CZK *(most popular)*
End-to-end service: consultation on client needs, active vehicle sourcing, pre-selection of candidates, inspection accompaniment, professional examination, price negotiation, and administrative support.

### Custom Services — price negotiable
Online inspections, technical compliance checks, vehicle transport, extended diagnostics, and other specialised requests. Travel charged at 10 CZK/km.

## Target Customer

Used car buyers in the Czech Republic who want professional protection against buying a problematic vehicle — people who lack the technical knowledge to assess a car themselves or who want an independent second opinion before committing to a purchase.

## Unique Selling Points

- **Family automotive tradition since 1935** — nearly 90 years of hands-on experience
- **Established auto repair shop in Prague** — real workshop knowledge, not just theory
- **No upfront fees** — payment only after a successful purchase
- **Fairness and transparency** as core values — individual approach for every client
- **Combined expertise** — technical inspection skills paired with used car market knowledge
